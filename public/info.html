Origins:

Cyber discovery is a goverment endorsed program that is designed to engage school children from years 10-13 into the world of cyber defence. It is a four stage program currently being in its first. Due to the time between the first and second stage the Cyber Discovery Unofficial Discord was created and players joined creating their own challenges and even completing some additional ones from Cyber Discovery staff. This is why I created Challenge Master as a unified place to host these challenges and to also add to the competition by implementing a scoring system that allows points to be awarded even when the challenge host is offline.

Rules:

The rules are simple DO NOT CHEAT and if you find a way to cheat report it responsibly. Cheating in any of form will result in a ban.
A non exclusive list of cheating methods:
  • Creating Multiple Accounts to gain score.
  • Creating challeges that do not actually lead to the given solution.
  • Telling people the accepted solution to a challenge, although hints are fine

Points are awarded as follows:
  • 10 Points are awarded to the first person to solve a challenge.
  • 5 Points are awarded to any user who reports a security vulnerbility in the website, see info. - I reserve the right to decide what counts as "a security vulnerbility"
  • 2 Points are awarded to users who solve a challenge but are not the first to do so.
  • 1 Point is awarded a user who created a challenge when it is solved for the first time.
  • I reserve the right to award and revoke score, however I will disclose when this happens

Bug Bounty:

As seen above one way of earning points is by disclosing security flaws in the website to me. This is to be done via discord DM's and you are not allowed to discuss the nature of the flaw until I tell you that it has been fixed. I will decide whether your security flaw is worthy of score on a case by case basis. However, I will not award points for showing me that the site can be DDOSed so please do not do this as if I go over a traffic limit the site will shutdown. Additonally, do not attempt to mess with the site in a manor that will effect the end users experiance. Finally, as well as being rewarded with score your name will be put into the White Page of Fame where you will be regarded as a demi-god second to only the saviour himself.
